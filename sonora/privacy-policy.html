1. What we listen for

Your voice recordings

When you share your thoughts with Sonora, we listen carefully and purposefully:

  • Transcription: Your audio is sent to our secure transcription service to convert speech to text
  • AI Analysis: Transcribed text is analyzed to generate summaries, extract themes, and identify action items
  • Local Storage: Audio files and transcriptions are stored locally on your device

Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information to improve app performance:

  • App version and iOS version for compatibility
  • Crash reports (anonymized) to fix bugs
  • Performance metrics to optimize the app

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or other device data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality

  • Transcription: Audio recordings are processed to provide accurate text transcription
  • AI Analysis: Transcripts are analyzed to generate summaries, themes, and actionable insights
  • App Features: Data is used to enable Live Activities, search functionality, and memo organization

Service Improvement

  • Improve transcription accuracy and speed
  • Enhance AI analysis quality
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance

Lawful Basis (EU/UK)

Where applicable, we process your data under the following lawful bases:

  • Performance of a contract: To provide the core app functionality you request
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain and improve app performance and security
  • Consent: For microphone access and optional features you explicitly enable

3. Keeping your voice safe

Your thoughts stay with you

Your recordings and transcriptions live primarily on your device, where they belong, protected by iOS's thoughtful security.

Secure Processing

When audio is sent for transcription and analysis:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption
  • Processing occurs on secure servers with limited retention
  • Audio files are deleted from our servers after processing
  • No permanent copies are stored on our servers

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you delete it
  • Server Processing: Audio data is deleted immediately after transcription
  • Technical Logs: Anonymized logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Sonora uses the following third-party services:

  • Transcription Service: Secure API for converting speech to text (audio data only, no personal information)
  • AI Analysis Service: Text-only analysis for generating insights (no audio data shared)

Service Providers (Processors)

We only engage trusted service providers under written data protection terms. Providers are limited to processing your data on our instructions and only as necessary to deliver the service. We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising.

No Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your recordings with advertisers
  • Use your content for marketing purposes
  • Provide data to social media platforms

5. Your Rights and Controls

Data Control

  • Deletion: You can delete any recording and its transcription from within the app
  • Export: You can export your data using iOS sharing capabilities
  • Access: All your data is accessible directly within the app

Privacy Settings

  • Microphone access can be revoked in iOS Settings
  • Live Activities can be disabled in iOS Settings
  • Background processing can be controlled in iOS Settings

Your Legal Rights (where applicable)

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Portability of data you provided
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sonora-app.com. We do not discriminate against users for exercising privacy rights.

6. Children's Privacy

Sonora is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. International Users

Sonora is designed to work globally, but our servers are located in the United States. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your data to the United States for processing, subject to this Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date below
  • Material changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@sonora-app.com
  • Support: Sonora Support Page
