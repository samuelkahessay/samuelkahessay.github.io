üöÄ Getting Started with Sonora

System Requirements:

  • iOS 17.0 or later for full feature compatibility
  • iPhone (optimized for iPhone 15 Pro with Dynamic Island)
  • Microphone access permission for voice recording
  • Stable internet connection for transcription and AI analysis
  • At least 100MB of free storage space
  • Age Rating: 9+ (App Store content rating)

Your First Steps with Sonora

  1. Download & Install: Get Sonora from the App Store and install on your iPhone
  2. Grant Permissions: Allow microphone access when prompted for voice recording
  3. Start Recording: Tap the elegant record button to create your first voice memo
  4. Explore AI Features: Try transcription and discover insights in your recordings
  5. Customize Settings: Adjust Live Activities and notification preferences

Understanding the Interface

Sonora follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines and feels natural on iPhone. The recording interface includes a record button, real-time waveform, and Dynamic Island integration.

üìù Transcription & Text Magic

Sonora offers two transcription modes:

  • Cloud Transcription: You get 10 minutes per day of cloud‚Äëbased transcription. Audio is sent securely for processing and deleted after completion.
  • Local Transcription (On‚ÄëDevice): Unlimited. Audio is processed on your iPhone and stays on your device.

Sonora supports transcription in many languages and dialects, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese (Mandarin & Cantonese), Japanese, Arabic, Portuguese, and Russian. The service can automatically detect the spoken language in most cases.

Transcription accuracy depends on several key factors:

  • Audio Quality: Clear speech in quiet environments can improve results
  • Speaking Pace: Normal, conversational speed is ideal for accurate transcription
  • Language Support: Some languages may have higher accuracy rates than others
  • Technical Terminology: Specialized vocabulary or jargon may be less accurately transcribed
  • Accents & Dialects: Strong regional accents might affect transcription accuracy
  • Background Noise: Ambient sounds can interfere with speech recognition

Currently, Sonora displays transcriptions as generated by our advanced AI service to maintain the integrity of the original analysis. If you need to make corrections, you can easily copy the text and edit it in your preferred text editor. We're actively considering adding in-app editing capabilities in future updates based on user feedback and requests.

Transcription time depends on connection, server load, and content. The process starts automatically after recording, and a progress indicator is shown.

üîí Privacy & Data Protection

Your voice belongs to you, and we take that responsibility seriously. Sonora is built with privacy-by-design principles, ensuring your personal thoughts and recordings are protected at every step.

How We Protect Your Voice

  • On-Device First: Recordings and transcripts are stored locally on your iPhone unless you choose to export them
  • Secure Processing: Audio is sent to our secure, encrypted transcription service and permanently deleted after processing
  • Text-Only Analysis: AI insights are generated from transcribed text only‚Äînever from your original audio
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We don't use analytics services that track your behavior or sell your data
  • Complete Control: You can delete any memo and its associated data at any time with a simple swipe
  • Transparent Processing: Clear indicators show when your data is being processed and when it's complete

Data Retention Policy

  • Local Storage: Your recordings remain on your device indefinitely until you choose to delete them
  • Server Processing: Audio files are automatically deleted from our servers within minutes of transcription completion
  • No Permanent Cloud Storage: We never store your voice recordings in the cloud permanently
  • Anonymized Logs: Technical logs for debugging are anonymized and retained for only 30 days maximum

For complete details about our privacy practices, please review our comprehensive Privacy Policy.

üîß Troubleshooting & Technical Support

Crash Resolution Steps:

  1. Force close Sonora and restart the app completely
  2. Verify you have sufficient storage space (minimum 100MB free recommended)
  3. Check microphone permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > Sonora
  4. Update to the latest version of Sonora from the App Store
  5. Restart your iPhone to clear any system-level conflicts
  6. If problems persist, contact our support team with your device model and iOS version

Network & Transcription Issues:

  1. Check your internet connection stability (both Wi-Fi and cellular)
  2. Try switching between Wi-Fi and cellular data to test connectivity
  3. Close bandwidth-intensive apps that might be competing for connection
  4. Wait a few minutes and retry‚Äîour servers may be experiencing high demand
  5. Ensure Low Power Mode is disabled, as it can throttle network requests
  6. Try recording a shorter test memo to isolate the issue

Live Activities Configuration:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Sonora and ensure "Live Activities" is enabled
  2. Check Settings > Face ID & Passcode > "Allow Access When Locked" > ensure "Live Activities" is turned on
  3. Verify you're running iOS 17.0 or later‚ÄîLive Activities require this minimum version
  4. Restart the Sonora app and attempt a new recording
  5. If you have a Dynamic Island-compatible device, check that Dynamic Island is enabled in settings
