üé§ Recording & Audio

Why is there a 3-minute recording limit?

‚ñº

Sonora is designed for quick voice memos and focused thoughts. The 3-minute limit encourages concise, actionable recordings while ensuring optimal performance for transcription and AI analysis. This also helps preserve battery life and storage space.

Can I record in the background or with the screen locked?

‚ñº

Yes! Sonora supports background recording with Live Activities. You'll see a real-time recording indicator in your Dynamic Island and on the lock screen. The recording will continue even if you switch apps or lock your device.

How can I improve recording quality?

‚ñº

üì¢ Tips for Better Audio:

  • Hold your iPhone 6-8 inches from your mouth
  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace
  • Find a quiet environment when possible
  • Avoid covering the microphone with your fingers
  • Ensure your device's microphone is clean and unobstructed

What if my recording fails or cuts off?

‚ñº

üîß Troubleshooting Steps:

  1. Check that microphone permissions are enabled in Settings > Sonora
  2. Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
  3. Close other apps that might be using the microphone
  4. Restart the Sonora app
  5. If issues persist, restart your iPhone

üìù Transcription & Text

What languages are supported for transcription?

‚ñº

Sonora supports transcription in 100+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and many more. The transcription service automatically detects the spoken language in most cases.

Why is my transcription not accurate?

‚ñº

Transcription accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Audio Quality: Clear speech in quiet environments works best
  • Speaking Pace: Normal, conversational speed is ideal
  • Language: Some languages may have higher accuracy than others
  • Technical Terms: Specialized vocabulary may be less accurate
  • Accents: Strong regional accents might affect accuracy

Can I edit transcriptions?

‚ñº

Currently, Sonora displays transcriptions as generated by our AI service. If you need to make corrections, you can copy the text and edit it in another app. We're considering adding in-app editing in future updates based on user feedback.

How long does transcription take?

‚ñº

Transcription typically takes 10-30 seconds for a 3-minute recording, depending on your internet connection speed and server load. The process happens automatically after recording, and you'll see a progress indicator during transcription.

üîß Troubleshooting

The app crashes when I try to record

‚ñº

üîß Try these solutions:

  1. Force close and restart the Sonora app
  2. Check if you have sufficient storage space (at least 100MB free)
  3. Verify microphone permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone
  4. Update to the latest version of Sonora from the App Store
  5. Restart your iPhone
  6. If the problem persists, contact support with your device model and iOS version

Transcription is taking too long or fails

‚ñº

üåê Network-related issues:

  1. Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular)
  2. Try switching between Wi-Fi and cellular data
  3. Close other apps that might be using bandwidth
  4. Wait a few minutes and try again (servers may be busy)
  5. Ensure you're not in Low Power Mode

Live Activities aren't showing up

‚ñº

üì± Check your settings:

  1. Go to Settings > Sonora and ensure "Live Activities" is enabled
  2. Check Settings > Face ID & Passcode > Allow Access When Locked > "Live Activities"
  3. Verify you're on iOS 17.0 or later
  4. Restart the app and try recording again
